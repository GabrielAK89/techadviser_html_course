The Paragraph tag

In HTML, the paragraph tag is part of the block level elements group. Block level elements will generally start on a new line and any Mark-up under or after the block level element will also start on a new line. Here is an example of a paragraph tag in HTML, followed by some text after the ending paragraph tag. Even though all of the text is on one line, the paragraph tag (block level element) will place the text after the closing paragraph tag on a new line.
